Ibrahim

Name: Ibrahim

Age: 54

Origin: Libya

Time Period in Jordan: 10 years

Story:Ibrahim struggles in Jordan and desires to leave at any moment he can. He struggled to put his kids in school until he found CRP which made him and his family feel welcomed and gave his family a sort of education. Nonetheless, although this helped Ibrahim and his family, they still feel left out from everyone else and seem to be uncomfortable when in public often getting asked many questions and getting looks. However, Ibrahim still tries to find the best possible outcomes and solutions for his family and make them happy.
