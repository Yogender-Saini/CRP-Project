Welcome to Refugee Assistance

CRP also known as collateral repair project is an organization that seeks to bring help to refugees inside of Jordan, Amman. CRP offers the community of refugees a way to process the trauma of displacement. They give opportunities for different income sources and a chance to rebuild their lives for the better.

CRP was first founded in 2006 by two American women who saw a need for direct connection with people fleeing war in Iraq. In 2018 CRP opened in the downtown center of Amman. This location is a great place as it is accessible to refugees from all over the world. Initially, CRP would mainly offer emergency assistance such as food vouchers and English classes for those refugees who would apply for resettlement. However, this did not work out as they were not getting many refugees to apply for resettlement which is why CRP switched to offering education, mental health, and wellbeing.
